# Privacy Policy for Soul Shelf

**Effective Date:** June 22, 2026

Welcome to **Soul Shelf** ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our mobile application. 

This Privacy Policy describes how **Soul Shelf** collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our household inventory management mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your information. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide, sync, and improve the features of Soul Shelf.

### A. Personal Data
* **Email Address:** When you log in or register an account with Soul Shelf, we collect and store your email address. This is required for user identification, syncing your inventory across devices, and securing your account.

### B. Device and Feature Data
* **Images and Camera Data:** If you use the receipt/bill scanning feature, the App requests access to your device camera to capture images of grocery bills. These images are processed to extract stock items.
* **Geographical Location:** If you use the nearby store suggestions feature, the App requests access to your high-precision (GPS) or low-precision (network-based) location.
* **Inventory Data:** We store the names, quantities, categories, and expiry dates of items you add to your virtual shelves or shopping lists.

*Note: Soul Shelf does **not** collect, store, or process credit card numbers or any other monetary payment information.*

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following operational purposes:
* **Account Syncing & Access:** To provide, maintain, and secure your personalized household inventory account via Firebase.
* **Feature Execution:** To run the automated grocery bill scanning (via Camera permission) and provide suggestions for local shopping destinations (via Location permission).
* **AI Assistance:** To analyze your inventory and generate helpful responses relative to your questions using Gemini AI.
* **Transactional Communications:** To send automated service emails (such as passcode resets, registration verifications, or security warnings) through Firebase.
* **App Optimization:** To understand user trends, troubleshoot performance bugs, and improve the inventory management workflow.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

To provide syncing, authentication, and intelligent assistance, Soul Shelf integrates with reputable third-party service providers. These third parties handle your data according to their own privacy standards:

* **Google Firebase (Authentication & Cloud Firestore):** Used to log users in securely and sync inventory items seamlessly across devices. Firebase may send automated administrative emails on our behalf. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
* **Google Gemini AI API:** Used to process your natural language questions regarding your shelves and items. No personal identity data is shared with the LLM API. 
* **Google AdMob (Future Integration):** While the App does **not** currently serve ads, we plan to use Google AdMob in the future to show relevant advertisements. AdMob may utilize anonymous device IDs for ad targeting.

We **do not** sell, trade, or share your personal user data with any other external third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

---

## 4. Device Permissions

To deliver specific features, Soul Shelf requires your explicit consent to access certain hardware functions. You can grant or revoke these permissions at any time via your device settings:

* **Camera Permission (`android.permission.CAMERA`):** Required to take pictures of store bills and receipts for automated inventory loading.
* **Location Permission (`android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION` / `android.permission.ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION`):** Required to locate your position to recommend nearby grocery stores and shops.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

Your data security is of paramount importance to us. 
* **Cloud Storage:** Your profile statistics, shelves, and lists are securely stored online in Firebase Firestore databases.
* **Information Protection:** We utilize commercial-grade security practices provided by Google Cloud Infrastructure to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or alteration of data.
* **Local Caching:** Part of your visual preference state (e.g., dark theme) is safely cached locally on your physical device.

While we take every reasonable precaution, please be aware that no security system or transmission over the internet can be guaranteed as 100% immune from malicious breaches.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Soul Shelf is a household organization tool designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collection information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with an email address or other personal credentials without your consent, please contact us immediately, and we will delete the account promptly.
